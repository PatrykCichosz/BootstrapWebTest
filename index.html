About Me

I am a dedicated software development student with hands-on experience, including a rewarding internship at Mastercard where I focused on backend system development and testing. My experience includes working with APIs for real and virtual card systems, using Java and Spring Boot to create unit, integration, and automation tests. The internship helped me hone my problem-solving skills and allowed me to collaborate in a team during an intern hackathon, developing innovative solutions.

Outside of tech, I have a diverse background. I’ve been active in sports, winning tournaments in football, basketball, table tennis, and boxing. Additionally, I played a key role in organizing charity events, including a fundraiser that raised over €2000 for the Simon Community, an organization supporting the homeless in Ireland. I believe in being a positive, cooperative, and competitive individual, always striving to achieve my goals while helping others. I’m also the elected maths tutor in my class, a role that has helped me develop teaching, patience, and problem-solving skills.
